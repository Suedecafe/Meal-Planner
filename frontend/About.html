Prep Savior's Mission

Prep Savior was founded on the idea that meal planning is not only hard, but stressful, nor fun. People needed a place to go to to help defer them from that burden and thus Prep Savior was born! With Prep Savior, users are able to interact with our straight-forward, colorized, aestheticly pleasing UI to create meal plans,delete unwanted meal plans, and utilize google maps to enhance there meal planning ventures
